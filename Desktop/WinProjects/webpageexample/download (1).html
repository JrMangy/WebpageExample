Nike Air Foamposite One ‘Northern Lights’ Releases Tomorrow

By
Brian Betschart
-
Feb 11, 2016
Facebook
Twitter
Google+
Pinterest
WhatsApp

    The Nike Foamposite One ‘Northern Lights’ will be apart of Nike Sportswear’s 2016 All Star collection. What is starting to become a yearly tradition, the latest Foamposite One will be inspired by where the games will be held, Toronto, Canada.

    The Northern Lights edition of the Foamposite One will celebrate All Star Weekend taking place in Toronto, Canada. Much like last years ‘Mirror’ release, the 2016 All Star Foams will be limited. The Foamposite shell will feature an array of green, blue and purple that represents Aurora Borealis. Black is then used on the eyestays, outsole and tongue while the laces, tongue and heel tab will use 3M reflective material. Both insoles display a Aurora Borealis mirage and are then completed with different color lace tips or purple and teal.

    Nike Foamposite One All Star Northern Lights Release Date

    You can expect the ‘Northern Lights’ Nike Air Foamposite One to release at Nike Sportswear accounts on February 12th, 2016 and will retail at $250. Make sure to check back with us for future updates and images. What do you think of this years All Star edition Foamposite One?
